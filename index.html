Who We Are

There when things go wrong

The DASBF has been quietly supporting ambulance staff and their families since 1990 — providing confidential financial assistance in times of hardship, bereavement, and severe illness.

Who We Support

For everyone who answers the call

  • ✓ All SWASFT operational staff based or residing in Devon — paramedics, technicians, and crew
  • ✓ Control room and non-operational DASBF members
  • ✓ Immediate family members of qualifying staff in bereavement cases
  • ✓ All support is completely confidential — your employer is never informed
