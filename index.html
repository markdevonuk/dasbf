Who We Support

For everyone who answers the call

  • ✓ All Devon Ambulance Trust operational staff — paramedics, technicians, and support crew
  • ✓ Control room and non-operational staff who are DASBF members
  • ✓ Immediate family members of qualifying staff
  • ✓ Support is always discretionary and completely confidential
