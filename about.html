Our Story

What is the DASBF?

The Devon Ambulance Staff Benevolent Fund is a charitable fund established in 1994 by Devon ambulance staff, for Devon ambulance staff.

We provide confidential financial assistance to members and their families who find themselves in times of genuine need — whether through illness, bereavement, accident, or other unexpected hardship.

What We Do

How the fund works

👔

Financial Assistance

Discretionary grants or interest-free loans to members facing genuine financial hardship — illness, bereavement, accident, or other serious difficulty.

🔒

Completely Confidential

Applications and awards are handled in strict confidence by the committee. Your colleagues, managers, and employer are never informed.

🆕

Funded by the Lottery

The weekly DASBF lottery is our primary funding mechanism. Every £1 ticket directly supports the benevolent fund and keeps it running.

ℹ

All assistance is at the discretion of the committee. The DASBF is not affiliated with Devon NHS Ambulance Trust and is entirely run by volunteers.
