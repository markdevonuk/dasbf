Who We Are

Supporting staff since 1990

Ambulance staff suffer the normal stresses and strains of everyday life. However, with such a demanding job comes additional pressures. The Devon Ambulance Staff Benevolent Fund is there when things go wrong, supporting staff and their families in times of hardship, bereavement, and severe illness.

Why not join more than 549 of your fellow ambulance staff colleagues who are members of DASBF for just £3 per month. You are eligible to apply if you are an employee of South Western Ambulance Service NHS Trust based or residing anywhere in Devon.

The DASBF can provide a valuable income in the form of hardship grants, death in service grants, and retirement awards. Members can also subscribe to the lottery by paying £2 per month in addition to their basic subscription.
