How It Works

Simple, weekly, and for a great cause

1

Sign up as a member

Membership is required to enter the lottery. It's quick to sign up online — just head to the Join page.

2

Pay £1 per week

Your lottery contribution of £1 per week (£52/year) is collected alongside your membership fee. You can enter multiple times to increase your chances.

3

Watch the weekly draw

Numbers are drawn each week and results posted on the DASBF Facebook page. Winners are contacted directly.

4

Win cash prizes

Multiple prizes are available each week. Even if you don't win, your ticket goes directly towards supporting your colleagues.
