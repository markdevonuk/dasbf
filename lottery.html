How It Works

Simple, quarterly, and for a great cause

1

Join as a DASBF member

Basic membership is £3/month. You must be a member to enter the lottery.

2

Add the lottery — £2/month

Pay an additional £2/month (£24/year) on top of your membership subscription to enter every quarterly draw. You can enter multiple times to increase your chances.

3

Watch for the quarterly draw

Draws take place in February, May, August, and November. Results are posted on the DASBF Facebook page after each draw.

4

Win cash prizes

Winners are contacted directly by a committee member and can receive payment by cheque or bank transfer.
