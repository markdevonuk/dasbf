Eligibility

Who can join?

  • ✓ All current Devon NHS Ambulance Service Trust (DAST) staff — operational and non-operational
  • ✓ Paramedics, emergency medical technicians, and student paramedics
  • ✓ Control room and dispatch staff
  • ✓ Management, admin, and support staff employed by DAST
  • ✓ Bank and part-time staff who meet the DAST employment criteria

ℹ

Not sure if you're eligible? Contact any committee member — they'll be happy to help.

Why It Matters

You never know when you might need it

Emergency work takes its toll. The DASBF exists for those moments when life becomes unexpectedly difficult — illness, injury, bereavement, financial crisis.

For just £1 a week, you're both protected and protecting the colleagues beside you. That's what the DASBF has been doing since 1994.
