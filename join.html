Eligibility

Who can join?

  • ✓ All current South Western Ambulance Service NHS Trust (SWASFT) staff based or residing in Devon
  • ✓ Paramedics, emergency medical technicians, and student paramedics
  • ✓ Control room and EOC dispatch staff
  • ✓ Management, admin, and support staff employed by SWASFT
  • ✓ Bank and part-time staff who meet SWASFT employment criteria
ℹ

Not sure if you're eligible? Contact enquiries@dasbf.co.uk or speak to any committee member.
